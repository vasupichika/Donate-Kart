Donar Kart FAQ'S

1. What types of donations can I make?

You can make monetary donations, donations of goods or services, or donations of money, food or clothes.

2. How can I stay updated on the organization’s progress?

We will be sending our newsletter to your Email-ID through which you will be updated regarding our organization’s Donations.

3. How will my donation be used?

Your donation will be used to support our mission to help children in need by providing them with basic necessities and educational opportunities. We use donations to purchase items such as school supplies, clothing, food, and hygiene items for the children. We also use donations to fund our educational initiatives, such as tutoring and mentoring programs.

4. What methods of payment do you accept?

We accept all types of Payments which include UPI Payment, COD, Card Payments

4. How quickly will my donation be processed?

Your donation will be processed immediately.

5. Are there any additional fees associated with my donation?

No, We don't charge any extra charges with your donation.
